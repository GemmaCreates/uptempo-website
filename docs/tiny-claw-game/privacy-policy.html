Privacy Policy

Tiny Claw Game

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tiny Claw Game ("the App") handles information when you use the App. Tiny Claw Game is published by Uptempo Enterprises LLC.

Information We Collect

Tiny Claw Game is designed as a private, kid-friendly play experience. The App does not require an account and does not collect personal information from children.

The App may store gameplay progress or settings locally on the device so the experience can work properly.

Support Emails

If a parent or guardian contacts us for support, we receive the information included in that email, such as an email address, device details, and the support question. We use that information only to respond to the support request.

Ads and Tracking

Tiny Claw Game does not use third-party advertising and does not use third-party tracking for behavioral advertising.

Purchases

If Tiny Claw Game offers in-app purchases, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store payment card information.

Children's Privacy

Tiny Claw Game is designed for family use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose information only if required by law or as needed to respond to a support request initiated by a parent or guardian.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
